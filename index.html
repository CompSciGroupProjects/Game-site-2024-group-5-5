Rock-Paper-Scissors

Two players have the choice between 3 symbols. One symbol will beat the other.

Tic-Tac-Toe

A 3 by 3 box which will be filled with X's and O's. Which ever player gets 3 in a row wins.

Connect-4

A game where colored chips are dropped into a grid, whichever player gets 4 in a row first wins.
