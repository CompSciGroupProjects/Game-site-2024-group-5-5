Connect 4 Rules

In Connect 4, two players choose between two colored discs. These discs drop from the top of the board, and are stacked on top of each other. The key to win is to get 4 in a row; this includes horizontally, vertically and diagonally. The rules are simple, the two players take turns dropping discs into the board while playing strategic moves in order to prevent their opponent from winning. Are you ready to play?
